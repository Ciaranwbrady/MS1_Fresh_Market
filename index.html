About Us

Family Owned

The Fresh Market is proudly owned by the Ryan family for 15 years. We treat our customers like family and love this community.

Independent

As we are Independent that means we choose the produce. We look to stock fair trade products that empower producers.

Coffee and Deli

We offer freshly brewed Upside Coffee daily, along with sandwiches and baked goods from Firehouse Bakery.

Quality Products

Stocking award winning products like: Harry's nutbutter, Upside Coffee, Peanut Rayu or Lilliput Trading Co. Olive Oil and Olives.
